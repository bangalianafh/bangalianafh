About

Who we are?

About of our Company

Bangaliana is a Bangladeshi Boutique Fashion Company established in 2014 and managed by a team expert in making Bengali traditional dresses. We have been working in the fashion industry for 6 years now We design and produce our own products in our office and factory both located in Dhaka City. Our entire production is proudly made in Bangladesh. Because of our expertise in manufacturing, we produce Boutiques for various brands all over Bangladesh and support them in developing their collections.
